# Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

This repository contains my research for the 100Hires hiring assessment, focused on building a practical Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS.

## What I Collected

- A curated list of B2B SaaS sales experts (`research/sources.md`)
- YouTube transcripts from selected experts (`research/youtube-transcripts/`)
- LinkedIn posts from selected experts (`research/linkedin-posts/`)
- Additional supporting materials (`research/other/`)

## Why I Chose These Experts

I selected practitioners with proven experience in B2B SaaS sales, outbound prospecting, GTM strategy, and enterprise selling.

Instead of choosing experts based on popularity, I prioritized professionals who actively share practical frameworks, real-world experiences, and actionable strategies that can contribute to building a Cold Outreach Pipeline playbook.

Together, these materials cover the major stages of a modern B2B SaaS Cold Outreach Pipeline:

- Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — Kyle Coleman
- Prospecting — Brandon Fluharty
- Signal-Based Outbound — Florin Tatulea
- Cold Email — Jason Bay
- Cold Calling — Morgan Ingram
- LinkedIn Outreach — Richard van der Blom
- Follow-up Strategy — Jen Allen-Knuth
- Negotiation — Armand Farrokh
- Enterprise Sales — John Barrows
- Closing — Chris Orlob

Together, these resources provide a structured knowledge base that can be used to develop a practical Cold Outreach Pipeline playbook for modern B2B SaaS sales teams.
