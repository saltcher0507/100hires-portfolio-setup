## Summary

Morgan Ingram shares three practical changes that helped an SDR team significantly improve their cold calling performance. He emphasizes building consistent calling habits, preparing for objections in advance, and using customer stories to create more engaging conversations that lead to more booked meetings.

## Key Takeaways

- Schedule dedicated cold calling blocks to build consistency and reduce hesitation.
- Document common objections and practice responses until they become natural.
- Use customer success stories to build trust and make conversations more compelling.
- Consistent preparation and structured execution improve meeting booking performance.
