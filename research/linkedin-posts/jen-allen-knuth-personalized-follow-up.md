## Summary

Jen Allen-Knuth explains how to make follow-up messages more memorable after an initial sales conversation. Instead of relying on generic subject lines or repeated check-ins, she recommends using personal details from previous interactions to reconnect with prospects in a natural and human way.

## Key Takeaways

- Personalize follow-up messages using details from previous conversations.
- Generic subject lines are easier to ignore than memorable, context-based ones.
- Being professional does not mean removing personality from your outreach.
- Keep the message concise while focusing on the prospect's business goals.
